Recipes Notebook

The Recipes Notebook is a web application that allows you to easily save, organize and search your favorite recipes. The application provides a clean and user-friendly interface for adding and editing your recipes, which can include ingredients, instructions, notes, and images. You can also search for recipes by keyword or ingredient, and filter your results based on various criteria. The Recipes Notebook is built using modern web technologies, such as Django, Docker, Bootstrap, and PostgreSQL, and is fully responsive and mobile-friendly. Whether you're a professional chef or a home cook, the Recipes Notebook is the perfect tool for keeping your recipes organized and accessible.

Fishbook

Fishbook is a web application for anglers who want to discover new fishing spots and share their catches with others. The app allows you to view and add information about different lakes, including location, fish species, and tips for catching them. You can also create a personal fishing log to keep track of your catches and share them with the community. Fishbook is built using modern web technologies such as Django, PostgreSQL, and Docker, providing a scalable and reliable platform for users. With its user-friendly interface and community-driven approach, Fishbook is the perfect tool for passionate anglers looking to connect with others and improve their fishing experience.
