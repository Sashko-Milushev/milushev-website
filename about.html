About Me

For the past 16 years, I have worked as a chef in the culinary industry, which was a passion of mine. However, as I started a family, I realized that the long and irregular hours of the industry made it difficult to balance work and family life. This motivated me to pursue a career change and explore my interest in programming. I am excited to bring my creativity and problem-solving skills to the tech industry, and to have a career that allows me to spend more time with my loved ones.

At first, it was just an experiment to see if I would be interested. But after delving into Python and discovering the limitless possibilities of programming, I fell in love with it. I realized that I had found my new passion, and I was eager to pursue it.

Since then, I've been constantly learning and growing my skills. I'm proficient in Python, JavaScript, Django, SQL, and OOP, and I'm always looking to expand my knowledge. I'm a detail-oriented person who enjoys analyzing everything around me, and I'm able to manage my time effectively.

Outside of programming, I enjoy spending time in nature. Hiking, camping, and fishing are some of my favorite activities. I also have an interest in cooking and DIY house repairs. I'm happily married with a baby daughter, and spending time with my family is very important to me. Honesty and doing everything with heart are values that I hold dear. I believe that if you work hard and are a good person, good luck will eventually come your way.

My goals for the future are to continue developing my skills and to take on more responsibilities in a tech company. I'm confident that I have the ability to make a valuable contribution to any team, and I'm committed to learning new technologies and gaining new skills. Ultimately, I hope to become a Senior Developer and inspire others to follow their passions, just as I was inspired by Senior Developers.
